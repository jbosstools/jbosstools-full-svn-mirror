General
Support for Eclipse 3.7 Indigo Our WTP facet for GWT was not included in JBoss Tools 3.3.x in the past since Google had not released their plugins for Eclipse 3.7 Indigo yet. In the meantime Google released a new version and we therefore updated our facet. We now fully support the GWT facet for dynamic web projects in Eclipse 3.7 Indigo.

You'll find installation and usage instructions in all details at http://community.jboss.org/wiki/CreateGWTProjectsWithJBossToolsAndEclipseWTP
